What Are Conjoined Twins?

Conjoined twins are identical twins whose bodies are physically connected. This rare phenomenon occurs when the embryo only partially splits during early development.

How Do They Form?

Normally, an embryo splits into two between days 5 and 8 after fertilization. If the split happens between days 13 and 15 and is incomplete, conjoined twins can form.
